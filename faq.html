Common Questions

How do I get a lot of tokens?

Each gamemode gives you a different amount of tokens per question. In general, the Gold Quest or Factory gamemodes are best for getting lots of tokens due to their fast pace nature. You can find a chart of each gamemode's tokens per question HERE. In these 2 mentioned gamemodes, you need to answer 400 questions correctly to get the max tokens per game. The time it takes can vary from 15-30 minutes depending on your answering speed. The most tokens you can get in one day is 500.

How do I get more Blooks?

All of the currently available Blooks are on the Market. Purchase Blook Boxes to get a chance of getting a new Blook. More Blooks are available for limited time for around a month on holidays such as Halloween and the Winter Holidays.
